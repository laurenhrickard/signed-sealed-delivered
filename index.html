About Us

Located in Kitsilano's West 4th Avenue shopping district, Signed Sealed Delivered is your go-to place for greeting cards, gifts, novelty items, stocking fillers, stationery, party items and more. We pride ourselves on offering the best selection of greeting cards in Vancouver for every occasion.

Our helpful team are on hand to answer any questions you might have, with the goal of helping you find the perfect card or gift, every time.
